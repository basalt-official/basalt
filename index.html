How it works

You don't leave the codebase to make it beautiful

01

Point at the preview

Click anything in your running app — text, a card, an image — the same as you'd click it in Canva.

02

Move, resize, restyle

Drag it where you want, resize it, change its color or spacing, right on the canvas.

03

Code updates, not a copy

Basalt edits your real source file in place — your styling system, your formatting, untouched.
